Book of Programming

This website contains fragments from the Book of Programming, an ancient tome lost to time.

The book is said to have been written by a disciple of Fu-Tzu, the legendary master programmer.

Sections

Currently, two sections have been recovered:

  • The Two Aspects of Programming a section describing the two aspects of programming (data and control);
  • The Wisdom of Fu-Tzu a section reporting short-sayings and other wisdom of the master Fu-Tzu.
